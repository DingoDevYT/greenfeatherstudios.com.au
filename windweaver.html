Harness the power of the wind in this upcoming high-speed indie adventure.

Grounded, But Not Giving Up

Jonathan has lost his ability to fly, but that isn't going to stop him. Windweaver is a high-speed, boost-style action game that challenges you to harness the currents of the air to make up for what you've lost.

Navigate treacherous, breathtaking environments and discover secrets hidden across the world, all while keeping your momentum alive.

Dash Through the World Below

Speed is everything. Without wings to carry you, you'll rely on pure skill and the world around you to chain together massive dashs, blitz across huge gaps, and outmaneuver foes.

Master the rhythm of the sloped terrain to maintain your top speed and uncover the world's deeper truths - wrapped in challenging, fast-paced stages.

The Journey Ahead

Windweaver is currently in active development by the dedicated team at Greenfeather Studios. We are pushing hard to refine the core dash mechanics and polish our 3D environments.

Stay tuned for development roadmaps, early alpha footage, and major milestone announcements as we ready ourselves for a Kickstarter and Steam Demo release!
